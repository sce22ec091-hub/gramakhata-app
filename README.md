# Grama-Khata

Grama-Khata is a rural digital ledger application developed for small shopkeepers to manage customer credit transactions efficiently. The application replaces traditional paper-based “Vahi” books with a secure digital system for recording dues, tracking payments, sending reminders, and maintaining customer records through a simple Android application.

## Introduction

In many rural areas, shopkeepers maintain customer credit details using handwritten ledger books. These traditional methods are time-consuming, difficult to manage, and prone to errors or loss of records. Grama-Khata provides a digital solution that simplifies credit management and improves record accuracy.

The application helps vendors store customer information, manage dues, record payments, and monitor transaction history in an organized and secure manner. It is designed with a simple interface so that users with minimal technical knowledge can easily use the application.

## Features

* Customer management system
* Credit and payment tracking
* Pending dues monitoring
* Transaction history maintenance
* SMS and WhatsApp payment reminders
* Secure digital record storage
* Simple and user-friendly Android interface

## Objectives

* To digitize rural credit management
* To replace traditional paper ledger systems
* To improve transparency in transactions
* To reduce errors in manual bookkeeping
* To support small shopkeepers with easy financial management

## Modules

### Customer Management

Stores and manages customer details digitally.

### Credit Transactions

Records customer dues and payment updates.

### Reminder System

Sends reminders for pending payments through SMS or WhatsApp.

### Transaction History

Maintains complete records of all transactions.
